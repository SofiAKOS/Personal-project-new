About the CCA Conference

Welcome to the Climate Change Awareness Conference 2020 (CCAC 2020) - to be held 28 May, 2020 in Rockfeller Center in New York, USA. The event is organized by Global Nest - an international association of scientists, technologists, engineers and other interested groups involved in all scientific and technological aspects of the environment, as well as in the application of methods aimed at sustainable development.

The conference will feature speakers representing both academia and industry who are experts in Climatology and the environmental issues. Thus, CCAC 2020 unites diverse researches and case studies from all over the world. It also offers many networking and publishing opportunities.
